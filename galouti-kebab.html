Galouti Kebab

Galouti kebabs are so tender that they melt in our mouths. They are the best choice to experience a burst of Awadhi cuisine flavors that are fabulous. Just imagine how finely grounded mutton or chicken keema seasoned with a bouquet of spices and herbs is laced with the smokiness of desi ghee, nuts, and finished with the thickness of the best quality cream.

Cooking Time : 15 min

Prep Time : 40 min

Total Time : 55 min

Servings: 3

Ingredients
  1. Half kg Mutton Kheema
  2. Two tbsp raw papaya
  3. Salt to taste
  4. One tbsp ginger
  5. One tbsp garlic
  6. Two black cardamom
  7. Eight cloves
  8. One tbsp poppy seeds
  9. Six pepper corns
  10. Half tsp cinnamon
  11. Half blade mace
  12. Six cardamom pods
  13. One tsp chilly powder
  14. Grated nutmeg
  15. One onion browned
  16. Half cup coriander leaves
  17. Four-Five dried green chillys
  18. Three tsp roasted gram flour
  19. One egg
Directions
  1. First Step
    • Grind together – bay leaf, black cardamom, cinnamon, dried green chilly, grated nutmeg, mace, peppercorns, poppy seeds, cloves, cardamom seeds, garlic, ginger, chilly powder, fried onion, raw papaya with the skin.
  2. Second Step
    • Add coriander leaves & egg into the paste and run it till it turns into a smooth paste
  3. Third Step
    • Take the grounded meat Add the masala paste and evenly mix it with the meat.
  4. Fourth Step
    • Add a spoonful of roasted gram flour and mix it evenly and properly
  5. Fifth Step
    • Take a scoop of the mixture and form a patty shape and place it on a plate.
  6. Sixth Step
    • Refrigerate the patties for 10 minutes.
  7. Seventh Step
    • In a pan heat some oil on medium flame, and place the kebabs and fry them .
  8. Eighth Step
    • Garnish it with lemon juice, Masala onion rings and bunch of fresh mint.
  9. Ninth Step
    • The Galouti Kebabs are ready to be served!
