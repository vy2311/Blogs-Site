Popular Indian Recipes !!!

India has a very wide variety of cuisines and hence a very large number of Recipes. Here we would take a look at a the recipes of few very popular dishes. So get ready ............

Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is probably the most well known Indian dish around the world. It is also very very popular in India. It's made with boneless chicken and is extremely flavourful.

Samosa

Samosa is by far the most popular savoury snack dish from India. It's deep fried and has very crispy outer covering and is filled with deliciously spiced potatos and other vegetables.

Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi biryani (also known as Hyderabadi dum biryani) is a style of biryani originating from the kitchens of the Nizams of the erstwhile Hyderabad State with basmati rice and meat

Galouti Kebab

Tunday Ke Kabab, also known as Galouti kebab, is a dish made out of minced meat which has almost become synonymous with the city of Lucknow, India.It is a part of Awadhi cuisine. It is said to incorporate 160 different spices.

Chicken Chettinad

Chicken Chettinad or Chettinad chicken is a classic Indian recipe, from the cuisine of Chettinad in Tamil Nadu, India. It consists of chicken marinated in yogurt, and a host special spices with sesame oil.

Gajar ka halwa

Gajar ka halwa and carrot halwa is a carrot-based sweet dessert pudding made by placing grated carrots in a pot containing a specific amount of water, milk and sugar, cardamom and then cooking while stirring regularly
